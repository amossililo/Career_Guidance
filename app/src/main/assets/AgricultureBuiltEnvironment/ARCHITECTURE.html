ARCHITECTURE

Architecture is the discipline that is concerned with the planning, designing and the overseeing of the construction of the built environment (man-made environment and structures). It is the art and technique of designing and building as distinguished from the skills associated with construction. Architectures work in the construction, planning, and design phases of projects. In conjunction with other team members, the focus is on building structure and interior design. This includes heating, ventilation, air condition, electrical, fire protection, lighting, plumbing and other systems specific to the project. studying architecture may find one working as;

Residential architects

deals with residential buildings and customized houses design

Commercial architects

deals with the designing of commercial buildings such as shopping malls, airports, sports halls and stadiums, skyscrapers and townhouses

Interior designer

involves designing and styling the interior of an apartment, an office or a restaurant. Deep knowledge of materials, fabrics, color and basics of furniture design.

Landscape architects

- concerned with outdoor spaces like parks, gardens, neighborhoods, campuses and public spaces. They design these spaces to be efficient, engaging and harmonious with the environment, they decide the locations for buildings, walkways, greenery, they choose the materials for walkways, type of trees and shrubs for green areas, they have a strong background in urban horticulture.

Urban architects-

they employ specialized building architecture such as landscape design and green design- deals with scale of neighborhoods, districts and cities- they are in charge of grouping buildings, designing nodes, paths and strict networks, they give the city its shape and sense of order amidst the economy, politics and culture that they had to face in their operation.

Industrial architects

the architecture of an industrial facility can be vital to its functionality-they need a suitable envelope that aids the flow of industrial process taking place inside. Industrial architects design efficient and function able buildings that aids easy flow of industrial processes .

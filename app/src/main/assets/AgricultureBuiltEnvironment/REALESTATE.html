REAL ESTATE

Real estate is a discipline concerned with the use, valuation, management and development of land and property within the built environment. It focuses on the legal, economic, sociological and technical factors which affect the ownership of land and property. It is a program that prepares individuals to develop, buy, sell, appraise, and manage real estate.it Includes instruction in land use development policy, real estate law, real estate marketing procedures, agency management, brokerage, property inspection and appraisal, real estate investing, leased and rental properties, commercial real estate, and property management. Studying real estate may find one working as;

Property manager

makes sure a property either residential or commercial runs smoothly and is ultimately making money for whoever owns it.

Leasing consultant

makes sure there are tenants in the building. It involves marketing and negotiating-the need to get the word out about special or openings on their buildings and often throw promotion events.

Commercial leasing manager

assigned to offices or storefronts. Negotiates deals and transactions with businesses. Keeps an eye on changes in the market places as they impact the budget of business for various property needs.

Corporate real estate manager-

companies often have openings for someone to come on staff and manage the real estate of the company. Large brands need to lease office space and commercial space. Some companies have real estate buildings that they need an in-house person to manage

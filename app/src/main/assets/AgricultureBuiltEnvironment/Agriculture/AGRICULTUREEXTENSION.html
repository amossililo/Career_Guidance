AGRICULTURE EXTENSION

Agriculture extension is the discipline that deals with the application of scientific research and new knowledge to agricultural practices through farmer education. It is the transfer of agricultural information and technology to farmers and similarly transferring information from farmers to researchers. Agricultural extension officers are the intermediaries between research and farmers, they operate as facilitators and communicators, helping farmers in their decision-making and ensuring that appropriate knowledge is implemented to obtain the best results with regards to sustainable production and general rural development.

ANIMAL SCIENCE

Animal science is the study of biology of animals that are under the control of human kind. It is the production and management of farm animals. Animal science deals with the following areas of study;

  • Animal breeding
  • Food and fiber production
  • Animal agribusiness
  • Beef management
  • Dairy management
  • Swine management
  • Poultry production
  • Nutrition
  • Sheep or small ruminating animal management

CAREER OPPORTUNITIES

  • Animal feed manufacturing companies
  • Pharmaceutical industry
  • Animal charities such as rescues, conservation, training and rehoming
  • Medical research companies
  • International welfare organizations
  • Zoos and sanctuaries
  • Local government departments and animal enforcements
  • Academia tourism.

Computer Science

Computer science is the study of computers and computer systems. It deals with software and software systems-this includes their theory, design, development and application. Computer scientists design and analyze algorithms to solve problems and study the performance of computer hardware and software. It is a program that focuses on computer theory, computing problems and solutions, and the design of computer systems and user interfaces from a scientific perspective. Includes instruction in the principles of computational science, computer development and programming, and applications to a variety of end-use situations. Computer science has created more billionaires than any other profession. Studying computer science may find one working as a:

  • Data scientist
  • Software engineer
  • Web developer
  • Product manager
  • Systems analyst
  • Network architect
  • Business analyst
  • Software tester
  • Information systems manager
  • Database administrator
  • Multimedia programmer

ABROAD SCHOLARSHIPS

Higher education loans and scholarship board administers scholarships received from friendly nations at Undergraduate, Masters and PHD levels. Selection of candidates is based on academic merit. Further HELSB administers scholarships from the common wealth scholarship commission for studies tenable in the United Kingdom at Masters and PHD levels.

Currently HELSB administers scholarships for study abroad from the following countries;

1. Algerian Student Scholarship

2. Cuban Student Scholarship

3. Cyproit Students Scholarship

4. Czech Republic Student Scholarship

5. Egyptian Student Scholarship

6. Indian Student Scholarship

7. Serbian Student Scholarship

Method Application

Applicants should submit;

1. Letter of application responding to the advertisement, clearly starting the program of study desired, age, day-time phone number for easy communication.

2. Curriculum Vitae, certified photocopies of applicants Green National Registration Card(NRC), copies of statement of results, Birth certificate and two (20)color passport size photos and a color photocopy of pages 2 and 3 of applicants passport.

3. A study plan or synopsis summarized details of preferred course and how the intended course of study will enhance ones contribution to Zambiaâ€™s development agenda (200 words for undergraduates and 400 words for postgraduates on one face of an A4 page).

4. It is a requirement by the host countries that all nominated candidates undergo comprehensive medical examination, including HIV, TB and Hepatitis B.

Precision Production Trades, Woodworking

This study deals with woodworking.

i. Cabinetmaking and Mill work

A program that prepares individuals to apply technical knowledge and skills to set up, operate and repair industrial woodworking machinery, and to use such machinery to design and fabricate wooden components and complete articles.

ii. Furniture Design and Manufacturing

A program that prepares individuals to apply technical knowledge and skills to prepare and execute furniture design projects, assemble and finish furniture articles or subassemblies, repair furniture, and uses a variety of hand and power tools.

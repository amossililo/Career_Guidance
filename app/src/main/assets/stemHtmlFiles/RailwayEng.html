Railway engineering

Railway engineering is a multifaceted engineering discipline that deals with the design, construction, and operation of all railway transport systems. It encompasses a lot of engineering disciplines such as civil engineering, mechanical engineering, electrical engineering, computer engineering, industrial engineering and production engineering. It was introduced in 2017 in Zambia and it is only offered at the Copper belt University in the school of engineering. Like all engineering programs except for computer engineering, railway engineering is a 5-year program.

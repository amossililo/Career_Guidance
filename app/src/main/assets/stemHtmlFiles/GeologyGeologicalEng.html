Geology/Geological Engineering

A program that prepares individuals to apply mathematical and geological principles to the analysis and evaluation of engineering problems, including the geological evaluation of construction sites, the analysis of geological forces acting on structures and systems, the analysis of potential natural resource recovery sites, and applied research on geological phenomena. Geology is the study of the structure, evolution and history of the earth and its mineral and energy resources. It analyzes the processes that have shaped the earth, using rocks and minerals to reconstruct that history.

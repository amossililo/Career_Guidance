COMMUNITY DEVELOPMENT STUDIES

Community development is a practiced based profession and an academic discipline that promotes participative democracy, sustainable development, human rights, economic opportunities, equality and social justice, through the organization, education and empowerment of people within their communities. It seeks to empower individuals and groups with skills they need to effect change within their communities. It focuses on the role and forms of markets, states and civil society in the improvement of poverty and the progress of human wellbeing on individual, family, and community, regional, national and global level. Its impact and application extend to technical fields such as agriculture, medicine, business, information, communication and technology, engineering, forestry, climate change and mining.

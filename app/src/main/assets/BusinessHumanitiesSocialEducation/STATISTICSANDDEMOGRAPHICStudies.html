STATISTICS AND DEMOGRAPHIC STUDIES

Statistics and demography are the study of human populations especially with references to size and density, distribution, and vital statistics such as births, deaths, immigration and emigration and a regular census of population to provide information that is key to making sound decision about national policy.

It a program that focuses on the systematic study of population models and population phenomena, and related problems of social structure and behavior. Includes instruction in population growth, spatial distribution, mortality and fertility factors, migration, dynamic population modeling, population estimation and projection, mathematical and statistical analysis of population data, population policy studies, and applications to problems in economics and government planning.
